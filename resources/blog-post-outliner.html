Types of Blog Post Outlines

üìù How-To Guide Outline

‚ñº

Structure:

  • Introduction: Problem statement
  • What You'll Need: Tools/requirements
  • Step 1: First action
  • Step 2: Second action
  • Step 3-N: Additional steps
  • Tips & Tricks: Pro advice
  • Conclusion: Summary and next steps

Best for: Tutorials, guides, instructional content

üìã Listicle Outline

‚ñº

Structure:

  • Introduction: Why this list matters
  • Item #1: Description and details
  • Item #2: Description and details
  • Item #3-N: Additional items
  • Conclusion: Key takeaways

Best for: Top 10 lists, resource roundups, comparison posts

üîç Problem-Solution Outline

‚ñº

Structure:

  • Introduction: Identify the problem
  • Why This Problem Matters: Impact and consequences
  • Common Causes: Root issues
  • Solution #1: First approach
  • Solution #2-N: Additional solutions
  • Implementation: How to apply solutions
  • Conclusion: Expected results

Best for: Problem-solving content, case studies

Pro Tip: Mix and match outline types based on your content needs. A how-to guide can include listicle elements, and a problem-solution post can incorporate step-by-step instructions.
