The Art & Science of User Research

2. The Power of Personas

A persona is not just a fictional character; it's an archetype based on real data. It helps your team develop empathy. Instead of saying "the user might like this," you say "Sarah (our persona) wouldn't have time for this feature."

3. Usability Heuristics

Jakob Nielsen defined 10 general principles for interaction design. These are the "rules of thumb" that every great interface follows. The most critical ones for modern apps are:

  • Match between system and real world: Speak the user's language, not system jargon.
  • User control and freedom: Always provide a "back" button or "undo" option.
  • Error prevention: A good design prevents problems from occurring in the first place.
